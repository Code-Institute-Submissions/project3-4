About AV Empire


AV Empire is a small company established in 2018 that primarily provides access to many of the latest Audio-Visual products through its online web store at very competitive prices. Our partners are some of the world's leading suppliers in AV communication, such as Sony, 3M, Bose and AMX.

The company currently operates just throughout Scotland, however we aim to expand our operations throughout all of the UK within the next year.
